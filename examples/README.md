# Example Ledgers

Illustration-only sample ledgers for CPA Stack.

## Business Samples

- `canadian-company/` — Canadian incorporated consulting business
- `usa-company/` — US freelance consulting business

## Personal Samples

- `canadian-individual/` — salaried Canadian individual
- `canadian-family/` — Canadian family household
- `usa-individual/` — salaried US individual
- `usa-family/` — US family household

## Notes

- These are sample datasets, not tax advice.
- Business samples keep owner taxes and personal insurance outside the business P&L.
- Personal ledgers model household cash flow and net worth, not business deductions.
- Jurisdiction packs under each sample are placeholders for workflow illustration only.
