# CRA Source Map for Canadian Tax Prep

Last verified: 2026-04-03

Use these pages as the primary source set for Canadian tax-prep workflow. Prefer these over blogs, forum posts, or memory.

## Core due dates

- Personal income tax due dates: <https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/individuals/topics/important-dates-individuals.html>
  Notes: For 2025 taxes, CRA shows April 30, 2026 for most personal returns and payments, and June 15, 2026 for self-employed filing, with payment still due April 30, 2026.
- T2 filing due dates: <https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/businesses/topics/corporations/corporation-income-tax-return/when-file-your-corporation-income-tax-return.html>
  Notes: T2 returns are generally due six months after fiscal year-end.
- Corporation balance-due day: <https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/businesses/topics/corporations/corporation-payments/paying-your-balance-corporation-tax/balance-day.html>
  Notes: CRA says corporation income tax is generally due two months after year-end, with some qualifying corporations getting three months.
- GST/HST filing deadlines: <https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/businesses/topics/gst-hst-businesses/complete-file-return-business/when-file.html>
- Payroll remittance due dates: <https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/businesses/topics/payroll/remitting-source-deductions/how-when-remit-due-dates.html>
- T4 filing guide and due date: <https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/publications/rc4120/employers-guide-filing-t4-slip-summary.html>
  Notes: For most filers, the 2025 T4 filing due date shown by CRA is March 2, 2026.

## Personal return core income

- T4 slip: Statement of Remuneration Paid: <https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/individuals/topics/about-your-tax-return/tax-return/completing-a-tax-return/tax-slips/understand-your-tax-slips/t4-slips/t4-statement-remuneration-paid.html>
  Notes: CRA maps box 14 employment income to line 10100 and explains the related CPP/QPP and EI boxes.
- Tax slips overview: <https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/individuals/topics/about-your-tax-return/tax-return/completing-a-tax-return/tax-slips.html>
  Notes: Use when the return is mostly slip-driven and you need to verify which slips belong in the package.

## Working rules for this repo

- Use CRA pages to determine obligations, form sets, and due-date logic.
- Use the ledger jurisdiction pack for rates, thresholds, and any modeled computations.
- If CRA publishes a year-specific date and the skill output depends on it, cite the exact date and the page used.
- If an item is ambiguous after reading the relevant CRA page, escalate it for professional review instead of inventing an answer.
