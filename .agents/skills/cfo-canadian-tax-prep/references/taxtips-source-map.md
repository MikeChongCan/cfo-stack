# Taxtips Secondary Map for Canadian Tax Prep

Last reviewed: 2026-04-03

Use this file for checklist ideas and edge-case discovery only. Do not treat it as the final authority over CRA pages.

## Why use it

TaxTips is useful for:

- surfacing common filing workflow checkpoints
- showing adjacent topics that are easy to miss
- highlighting rental-property edge cases that deserve professional review

Do not use it to override CRA wording, due dates, or form instructions.

## Pages worth scanning

- Filing your Canadian income tax return: <https://www.taxtips.ca/filing-your-canadian-income-tax-return.htm>
  Why: broad filing checklist, return-prep flow, and reminders to gather slips before filing.
- Real estate and Canadian tax issues: <https://www.taxtips.ca/real-estate.htm>
  Why: rental-property issue map, change-in-use topics, partial-home rental prompts, and interest-deductibility links.
- Small business / self-employment: <https://www.taxtips.ca/small-business-income-tax.htm>
  Why: useful when a personal return also has side-business income.
- Site map: <https://www.taxtips.ca/site-map.htm>
  Why: fast way to discover nearby topics without browsing aimlessly.

## Topics this repo should borrow from TaxTips

- “Make sure you have all slips before filing” as a first-pass control for T4-driven returns
- rental-property edge-case scanning:
  - current vs capital treatment
  - change in use
  - rent out part of your home
  - property income vs business income
- using topic maps to produce a `needs confirmation` list instead of pretending the return is straightforward

## Topics to skip by default

- tax-credit optimization
- broad tax-planning calculators
- any rate table or threshold that the CRA or jurisdiction pack should supply directly
